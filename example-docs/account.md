# Account Management

## Resetting Your Password

1. Go to app.example.com/forgot-password
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
4. Click the link and enter your new password
5. Password must be at least 8 characters with one uppercase letter and one number

If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder. You can also try resending from the login page. If the issue persists, contact support@example.com.

## Changing Your Email

Go to Settings > Account > Email. Enter your new email address and confirm with your current password. A verification email will be sent to the new address. Your old email remains active until you verify the new one.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA at Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. We support authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS verification.

To set up: scan the QR code with your authenticator app, enter the 6-digit code to confirm. Save your backup codes in a safe place — you'll need them if you lose access to your authenticator.

## Deleting Your Account

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your projects, data, and team associations will be permanently removed after a 30-day grace period.

To delete: enter your password, type "DELETE" to confirm, and click the delete button. You'll receive a confirmation email.

## API Keys

Generate API keys at Settings > API Keys. Each key has a name and optional expiration date. You can create up to 10 active keys on Pro and 50 on Enterprise. Revoke keys immediately if compromised.
