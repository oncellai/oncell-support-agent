# Billing

## Pricing Plans

We offer three plans:

- **Free**: Up to 3 projects, 1 team member, 1,000 API calls/month
- **Pro** ($29/month): Unlimited projects, 10 team members, 50,000 API calls/month, priority support
- **Enterprise** ($99/month): Everything in Pro plus SSO, audit logs, custom integrations, 500,000 API calls/month, dedicated support

All plans include a 14-day free trial of Pro features.

## Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Payment is processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers.

## Changing Your Plan

Go to Settings > Billing > Change Plan. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You keep all features until the cycle ends.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month and sent to your billing email. Download past invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoice History.

## Refunds

We offer a full refund within the first 30 days of any paid plan. After 30 days, we offer prorated refunds for the unused portion of your billing cycle. To request a refund, email billing@example.com or contact support.

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can reactivate anytime within that window without losing data.
